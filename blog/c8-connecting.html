How did you find the loving kindness meditation?

I tried the Loving Kindness practice where you read the script about how people are so like you, and how they want everything that you want, and essentially to be happy. I found that this exercise worked really really well. Too often, we look at life through our own eyes, and never stop to think about what the other person or people in our life are going through. Be it your mum, wife, dad, friend, anyone - this exercise is great at enabling you to consider their feelings and wellbeing.
