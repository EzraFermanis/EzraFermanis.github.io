Daily Mindfulness

What did you learn from the Mindfulness conversation? How did the other find it?

I found the mindfulness conversation really really fulfilling. It lets you vent (so to speak) about what went on in your day and really felt like a good way to unload. It was also a good way to see what was on my Wifes' mind. She absolutely loved it and suggested we make it a daily thing every night.

Which of the three suggested techniques will you use for practice?

I think that it's beyond my control now. The Wife has spoken, and apparently it's now a daily practice in our household. I would like to also do the Walking meditation and be mindful of every step, breath, movement etc that I do, just because it's something that I can do myself.
