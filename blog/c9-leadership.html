Leadership

What is your experience of compassionate (or not) Leadership?

I have had to manage staff on multiple occasions. Some of those staff were family members - brothers, so I found it hard to manage them without getting frustrated. I noticed on both cases (both when I managed non-family members and family members) that using compassion worked far more effectively than getting angry and demanding action.
