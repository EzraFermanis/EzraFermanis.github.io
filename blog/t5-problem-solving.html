Have a go at explaining how recursion compares to iteration (In your own words)

Recursion takes longer then iteration and uses more memory. But recursion is more readible and easier to understand and maintain in the long run. Recursion runs the risk of 'call stack overflow' which is when there is too many stacks of data - though this is often unlikely. Recursion is seen as the more elegant approach. Iteration is alot quicker and simpler to write.
